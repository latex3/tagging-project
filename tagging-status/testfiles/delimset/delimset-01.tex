delimiter definition:
\newcommand{\comm}{\delimpair[{*,}]}
\[
\comm!{\comm{A}{B}}{C}
+\comm!{\comm{B}{C}}{A}
+\comm!{\comm{C}{A}}{B}
=0
\]

alternative representation:
\renewcommand{\comm}{\delimpair[{*;}]}
\[
\comm!{\comm{A}{B}}{C}
+\comm!{\comm{B}{C}}{A}
+\comm!{\comm{C}{A}}{B}
=0
\]
